Guava Smoothie

Description

This guava smoothie is a luscious pink coloured party drink. Impress your guests and indulge your taste buds with this refreshing beverage.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups readymade chilled guava juice
  • 5 tbs chilled coconut milk
  • 1.5 cups vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend till you get a smooth and frothy liquid.
  3. Serve chilled.
